Welcome to my corner of the internet. I'm Kurt, and this is where I'll occasionally write about building things, side projects, and whatever else is on my mind.

## Why a blog?

I've been building side projects for a while now — things like [redditmentions.com](https://redditmentions.com), [quiki.eu](https://quiki.eu), and [8mins.com](https://8mins.com). Each one teaches me something, and I figured it's worth writing some of it down.

## What to expect

Expect 3-5 posts a year. Short, practical, and honest. Topics might include:

- Building and launching side projects
- Things I've learned (often the hard way)
- Tools and workflows I find useful

> "The best time to start writing was years ago. The second best time is now."
